County Representatives

Mary Sackett

District 1. This includes the majority of the City of San Rafael and unincorporated communities Lucas Valley, Marinwood, and Santa Venetia.

Katie Rice

District 2. This includes the towns of Fairfax, Kentfield, Greenbrae, San Anselmo, and Ross.

Stephanie Moulton-Peters

District 3. This includes Sausalito, Floating Homes, Marin City, Tam Valley, Almonte, Mill Valley, Alto-Sutton Manor, Strawberry, Tiburon, and Belvedere.

District 4. This includes coastal west Marin from Dillon Beach to Muir Beach; inland areas including Nicasio, Chileno Valley, the San Geronimo Valley, and west Novato; and areas of east San Rafael, San Quentin, Corte Madera, Larkspur, and Mill Valley.

Eric Lucan

District 5. This includes the majority of the City of Novato and the unincorporated areas of Bel Marin Keys, Loma Verde, Black Point, Green Point, Rush Creek/Atherton Corridor, and Indian Valley.
